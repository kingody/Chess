
Moving Pieces:
--------------

 To move, type the coordinates of the piece you want to move followed by the coordinates of your desired destination.

	-Example: D7 D5

Special Commands:
-----------------

 HELP: Shows this message.

 SAVE: Saves the current state of the game at savefileX.chs.

 SAVEAS filename: Saves the current state of the game at filename.chs.

 LOAD filename: Imports the state of the game from *filename*.chs.
