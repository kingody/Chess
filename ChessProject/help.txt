
Moving Pieces:
--------------

 To move, type the coordinates of the piece you want to move followed by the coordinates of your desired destination.

	-Example: D7 D5

Special Commands:
-----------------

 HELP: Shows this message.

 SAVE: Saves the current state of the game.

 CASTLE RIGHT/LEFT: Swaps the King with the right/left Rook if the requirements are met

	-Requirements: Both pieces have never moved before, the squares between them are empty,
	 and the King is not in check for every square till his final destination
